    General Experience and Reflections
  • What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

    • The most challenging part of this project for me was managing the use of GitHub and GitHub Desktop, and ensuring that I met all deadlines. I was able to overcome this by meeting the deadlines and asking questions when needed.

  • What did you enjoy most about this project?

    • What I enjoyed most about this project was working with team even though sometimes it became frustrating when nothing was finished, I still really liked the ability to compelte a different website to the site-plan I had made.

  • What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

    • If I could do the project again I would ensure that I had a better way to communicate with my team members to ensure that everyone was on the same page and deadlines were met.

  • Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.

    • Yes, I feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience because I was able to understand the importance of communication and meeting deadlines in a team setting. I also learned how to use GitHub effectively for version control and collaboration. This will assist me in a business setting because I will be able to effectively lead a team to a common goal as the client sees fit.
